About Beauty Salon

Our experts have curated some of the most popular services at Salon, just for you. Take your pick and head to your nearest salon and get started on your makeover!

Beauty Salon, India’s first and leading chain of beauty salons, offers expert services in hairstyling, skin and beauty care. It is dedicated to the contemporary Indian woman’s exploration of the fine art of beauty. Known for our magical concoction of stunning make-up, fabulous hair and excellent skin services, we bring the backstage expertise and experience of Lakmé Fashion Week to modern Indian women through a team of over 3500+ highly trained professional stylists. Beauty Salons are dedicated towards the evolvement of the modern Indian woman and her exploration of beauty.Professionally trained hair and makeup experts with countless shows under our belt and outstanding skin services come together at Beauty Salon and offer an unforgettable experience.
